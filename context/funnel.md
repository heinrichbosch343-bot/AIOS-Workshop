# Business Funnel

> How BoschAI converts prospects into AIOS implementation contracts.
> Pipeline counts are pulled live from the clients database.

## Currency
ZAR

## Stages

### 1. Interested
Initial contact made — prospect has shown interest in AIOS.
- Interested Leads → pipeline_daily.interested

### 2. No Reply (Followup)
Outreach sent — waiting on response, follow-up needed.
- Awaiting Reply → pipeline_daily.no_reply

### 3. Meeting Booked
First discovery call scheduled.
- Meetings Booked → pipeline_daily.meeting_booked

### 4. Followup Meeting Booked
Second call booked — scope refinement or needs assessment.
- Followup Meetings Booked → pipeline_daily.followup_meeting_booked

### 5. Proposal Delivered
Scoped AIOS proposal with pricing sent to prospect.
- Proposals Delivered → pipeline_daily.proposal_delivered

### 6. Won
Contract signed — implementation in progress or complete.
- Active/Closed Wins → pipeline_daily.won

### 7. Contact Again
Not ready now — nurture for a future conversation.
- Nurture List → pipeline_daily.contact_again

### 8. Lost
Not proceeding — deal closed lost.
- Lost Deals → pipeline_daily.lost

## Monthly Targets
- Monthly Implementations: 2-3
- Revenue per Implementation: R80,000+
- Monthly Revenue Target: R160,000-R240,000
