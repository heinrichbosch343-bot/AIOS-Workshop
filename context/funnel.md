# Business Funnel

> How BoschAI converts prospects into AIOS implementation contracts.
> Pipeline counts are pulled live from the clients database.

## Currency
ZAR

## Stages

### 1. Prospect
New leads identified — initial contact made or referral received.
- Active Prospects → pipeline_daily.prospects

### 2. Assessment Booked
Prospect has scheduled an AIOS assessment call.
- Assessments Booked → pipeline_daily.assessment_booked

### 3. Assessment Delivered
Assessment completed — client understands the AIOS opportunity.
- Assessments Delivered → pipeline_daily.assessment_delivered

### 4. Proposal Sent
Scoped proposal with pricing and deliverables sent to client.
- Proposals Sent → pipeline_daily.proposal_sent

### 5. Active Build
Implementation in progress — fee received, build underway.
- Active Builds → pipeline_daily.active_build

### 6. Handoff Complete
Build delivered and signed off — implementation complete.
- Handoffs Complete → pipeline_daily.handoff_complete

## Monthly Targets
- Monthly Implementations: 2–3
- Revenue per Implementation: R80,000+
- Monthly Revenue Target: R160,000–R240,000
