# LinkedIn Posting Cadence

Heinrich's weekly content calendar. Keeps posting consistent without burning out.

---

## Weekly Schedule

| Day | Activity | Pillar Focus |
|-----|----------|-------------|
| Mon | Original post | AIOS Concept or AI That Works |
| Tue | Engage (comment on 5 posts) | — |
| Wed | Original post | Building in Public or Client Stories |
| Thu | Engage (comment on 5 posts) | — |
| Fri | Original post or short take | Business Owner Bandwidth |
| Sat | Rest or light engagement | — |
| Sun | Plan next week's content | — |

## Guidelines

- 3 original posts per week minimum
- 10+ meaningful comments per week
- Batch-draft posts on Sunday using `/ideas` and `/post`
- Post between 07:00-09:00 SAST for best reach
- Don't force it. Skip a day rather than post something hollow.

---

_Adjust as Heinrich finds his rhythm. The cadence should serve him, not the other way around._
