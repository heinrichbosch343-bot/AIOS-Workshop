# LinkedIn Content Pillars

These are the recurring themes Heinrich posts about. Each post should map to one pillar. The agent reads this file when drafting and suggesting ideas.

---

## 1. The AIOS Concept

What an AI Operating System is, why it matters, how it works in practice. The five layers (Context, Data, Intelligence, Automate, Build). Why custom beats generic.

**Target reader:** Business owner who's heard of AI but doesn't know where to start.

## 2. Building in Public

Real updates from building and running the agency. Wins, mistakes, numbers. What's working, what isn't. Revenue milestones, client count, lessons from delivery.

**Target reader:** Other founders, agency owners, people considering starting something.

## 3. AI That Actually Works

Practical AI applications vs hype. Specific examples over theory. What a daily brief actually looks like. How an auto-follow-up bot saves 2 hours a week. Concrete, not conceptual.

**Target reader:** Skeptical operator who's tired of AI hype and wants to see real output.

## 4. Business Owner Bandwidth

The operator trap: working IN the business vs ON it. How automation gives time back. The three KPIs (away-from-desk autonomy, task automation %, revenue per employee). Less staff, more leverage.

**Target reader:** Busy business owner drowning in admin and management.

## 5. Client Stories

What Heinrich is building for clients, lessons from delivery. Anonymized where needed. Before/after of a client's operations. Specific modules installed and their impact.

**Target reader:** Potential client considering an AIOS build.
