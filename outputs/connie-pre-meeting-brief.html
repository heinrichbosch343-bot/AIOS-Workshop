Our first conversation gave you a sense of what AIOS can do for a consulting-led business. This session is about getting specific to Osun Consulting — your workflow, your clients, your deadlines. Questions to Think About

At peak, how many reports or RFPs are you running simultaneously — and how do you currently keep track of all of them?

When you start a new annual report, how much of the structure, language, or content is reused from previous work — and how manual is that process?

What's the biggest source of delay in your projects — waiting for client inputs, internal review cycles, or the drafting itself?

If you had an AI assistant that knew your style and your clients — what's the first task you'd hand over without hesitation?
